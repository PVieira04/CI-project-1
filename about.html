The Process

During my decade of experience in education, I have found the most effecive ways of teaching Maths.


Having interviewed tutors and helped them improve their practice, I know how every tutor starts out... and I was no different.


Every tutor begins teaching Maths the same way. They would ask: "What are you doing in Maths lessons at the moment, and where do you need to feel you need to improve?"


This is a fine start, however many tutors stop there and every session is either doing homework together or the tutor watches as the student goes through questions that the tutor sets them.


This is the most ineffective use of a tutor's time.
